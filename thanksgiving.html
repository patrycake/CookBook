Thanksgiving Pumpkin Pie

Description

Classic Pumpkin Pie for the Thanksgiving Holiday tasty and nostagic to the last bite

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 can pumpkin puree
  • 1 can sweeted condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon pumpkin spice
  • 1 unbaked pie crust

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Combine eggs, pumpkin puree, sweetened condensed milk, and pumpkin pie spice in a large bowl and mix until combined.
  3. Combine eggs, pumpkin puree, sweetened condensed milk, and pumpkin pie spice in a large bowl and mix until combined.
  4. Fit pie crust into a 9-inch pie dish; pour pumpkin mixture into the crust.
  5. Place pie on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes. Reduce heat to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and bake until filling is set, 35 to 40 minutes.
